History

We were founded in 1998 in Gilbert, Arizona by a group of people in the community who wanted to make the lives of people in Africa better by providing them with meals to help reduce poverty.

About Us

We are a non-profit based in Arizona dedicated to defeating poverty one meal at a time.

Donations

Through your donation, we are able to provide meals to people in Africa to help alleviate poverty. Another way that we are able to drive down costs for the food is through corporate partnerships that we have that allow for them to give us a discount when we buy in bulk from them and donations of food in kind.
